Google Coding Challenge Tips

  1. Keep last 15 minutes for optimization.
  2. Try to find brut force solution first.
  3. Take 4-5 minutes to think about approach.
  4. Code whatever solution you get first and then try to optimizie it.
  5. Conceder bottlenecks and edge cases.
  6. Keep your challenge ID and passkey ready.
  7. Dont get nervous. Its just a test.
